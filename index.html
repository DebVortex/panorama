panorama is a free, bootstrap 3 based template for your dashboard application. It comes with the possibility to rearange the widgets via drag and drop or minimize them via a click on the specific button. You also have the possibility to choose between two designes: panorama by day or panorama by nicht. All the information are stored inside of cookies.
